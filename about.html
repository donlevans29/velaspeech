The story

A journey across three continents.

Velia's path into speech therapy began in Los Angeles, California, working with children with special needs in under-resourced communities. She saw firsthand how much a child's ability to communicate shapes everything — their confidence, their friendships, their place in the world.

"I kept meeting children who had so much to share. They just needed someone to meet them where they were."

That conviction took her across parts of South America and West Africa, where she worked with children and families in vastly different cultural and linguistic contexts. What she found everywhere was the same: children who wanted to connect, parents who wanted to help, and a universal human need to be heard and understood.

Those experiences shaped everything about how Velia practices today — her patience, her cultural sensitivity, her belief that therapy must be built around the whole child, not just a set of clinical benchmarks.

Areas of expertise

What Velia specializes in.

Fifteen years of clinical experience across diverse communities has shaped a practice built on evidence-based methods, cultural competency, and deep specialization in the areas Columbus families need most.

🧠
Executive Function
Specialized support for children navigating attention, organization, working memory, and the language skills that underpin academic success. A core specialty at Vela.
🗣️
Articulation & Phonology
Helping children produce sounds clearly so they can be understood — by strangers, teachers, and friends, not just the people who love them.
✨
Childhood Apraxia of Speech
Evidence-based, intensive treatment for CAS — one of the most misdiagnosed and undertreated speech motor disorders in young children.
💬
Early Language Development
Play-based, parent-involved therapy for toddlers and preschoolers building their first words, sentences, and social communication skills.
🤝
Social Communication
Supporting children in building the conversational and social language skills that help them make friends, navigate school, and belong.
📚
Language-Based Learning
Targeted support for children whose reading, writing, or academic performance is affected by underlying language processing differences.
