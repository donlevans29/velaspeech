Why summer

The most overlooked window
for speech progress.

Summer isn't just a break — it's one of the most powerful opportunities for speech development. Without school interruptions, children can build momentum faster than at any other time of year.

🗓️
Consistent, uninterrupted sessions
No snow days, field trips, or early dismissals pulling your child out of their rhythm. Summer allows the consistent weekly sessions that create lasting progress.
🚀
Arrive ready for the school year
Children who receive targeted support over the summer enter the fall more confident, more communicative, and better prepared for classroom demands.
🧠
Reduced anxiety, better learning
Lower-pressure summer environments help children take risks with communication that they wouldn't in the classroom. Play-based therapy thrives in this season.
