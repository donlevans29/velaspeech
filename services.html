Core Specialty
Executive Function

Executive function is the cognitive foundation for nearly everything a child does — planning, attention, working memory, flexible thinking, and self-regulation. When these skills are underdeveloped, it shows up in school performance, emotional regulation, and the ability to organize and express thoughts clearly. Velia's executive function specialty is one of the most sought-after services at Vela and a core focus of the practice.

✓
Difficulty staying on task or following multi-step directions
✓
Struggles to organize thoughts when speaking or writing
✓
Emotional outbursts when routines change or tasks feel overwhelming
✓
Difficulty with working memory — forgetting instructions mid-task
✓
Challenges with academic language and classroom participation

Motor Speech
Childhood Apraxia of Speech

One of the most misunderstood and misdiagnosed speech disorders. CAS is a motor speech disorder — the brain has difficulty coordinating the movements needed for speech. It requires intensive, specialized treatment from a clinician who knows it.

✓
Inconsistent sound errors — different each time
✓
Longer words are noticeably harder than short ones
✓
Limited progress with previous speech therapy
